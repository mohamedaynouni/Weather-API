About the Weather App

This Weather App provides real-time weather information for any city around the world. You can check the current weather, as well as forecasts for the next 10 days and the past 10 days. Simply enter a city or zip code, and get detailed weather reports, including temperature, wind speed, and humidity.

Our goal is to deliver accurate and easy-to-read weather data to help you plan your day ahead!
